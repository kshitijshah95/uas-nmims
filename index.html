About our design

Team UAS NMIMS, a student group that aims at developing and fabricating Unmanned Aerial Systems. Through detailed studies and practical experience in the field, we believe in researching and developing a highly advanced system by integrating advanced modules. Scylla 2k16 being our UAV for the 14th AUVSI's SUAS International Competition secured 5th mission rank of the 50+ teams that participated from all over the world.

We Advice

Our expertise lets us impart knowledge to those who need help in the field.

We Design

The design determines the quality of the end product by considering all the related factors.

We develope

Development being the most crucial stage has to be done with the right set of tools and skills both.

What we do

We conduct workshops and research in the field of drone technology so as to develope
a highly advanced drone capable of performing various tasks.

Autonomous Take-off

Just by a hand launch, the UAV's intelligent system adjusts it's parameters to move along it's coarse.

Autonomous Landing

Based on the landing waypoint, the UAV being highly stable glides its way to the ground.

Aerial Surveillance

Integrated with a fully functional imagery system, the system is capable of aerial surveillance.

Payload Delivery

Packages can be dropped from the UAV so as to successfully deliver it without human life risk.
